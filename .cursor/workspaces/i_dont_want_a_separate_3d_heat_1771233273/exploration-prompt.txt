# Explore Feature (Deep Dive Mode) - ENHANCED VERSION

**Time Budget:** 45-90 minutes MINIMUM for deep exploration.
**If you finish in less than 45 minutes, you didn't explore deeply enough.**

**Context:** You have a complete issue specification. Now you need to explore HOW to implement it before creating a step-by-step plan.

**Your Mission:** Conduct exhaustive technical exploration to answer every "how might we" question. This isn't planning yet - it's researching, prototyping, analyzing, and making architectural decisions.

---

## Phase 0: Understand Your Role in the Workflow

**This is Step 2 of 3:**
1. **Create Issue** (COMPLETE - the WHAT and WHY)
2. **Explore Feature** ← You are here (deep dive into HOW)
3. **Create Plan** (step-by-step implementation)

**Your job right now:**
- Research technical approaches
- Prototype critical paths
- Make architectural decisions
- Document trade-offs
- Identify risks and edge cases
- Answer ALL open questions
- Test assumptions
- Find the simplest path

**NOT your job right now:**
- Write the step-by-step implementation plan (that's next)
- Write production code (that's after planning)
- Solve every detail (plan will handle that)
